# AI Interview Coach

## Idea

Build an AI interview coach for new graduates and career switchers. The product should simulate realistic interviews, score answers, and generate improvement plans.

## Constraints

- Launch an MVP within one month
- Focus on Chinese-speaking users first
- Validate willingness to pay early

## Success Metrics

- 7-day retention
- Paid conversion rate
- Completed mock interviews per active user

## What This Case Should Expose

- Whether the product wedge is strong enough for job seekers
- Whether the MVP can stay focused instead of becoming a full career platform
- Whether marketing and monetization logic are aligned

## Good Demo Moments

1. Show the Go or Maybe verdict.
2. Ask marketing whether B2C should come before university partnerships.
3. Promote the answer to an intervention and show the plan diff.
