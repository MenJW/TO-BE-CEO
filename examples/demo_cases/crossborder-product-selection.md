# Cross-Border Product Selection Assistant

## Idea

Build an assistant for small cross-border ecommerce sellers that evaluates product opportunities, sourcing risk, and channel strategy before a seller commits inventory.

## Constraints

- Focus on Amazon sellers first
- Keep research cost low
- Output actionable selection recommendations instead of generic reports

## Success Metrics

- Weekly active sellers
- Conversion from candidate products to selected products
- Report reuse rate

## What This Case Should Expose

- Whether the product is a workflow tool or an intelligence product
- Whether finance and marketing assumptions can support a subscription model
- Whether the software scope should stay narrow in the MVP

## Good Demo Moments

1. Show research and finance alignment on risk.
2. Ask the board whether a services-led launch is better than SaaS-first.
3. Promote a board reply into an intervention and inspect the new scorecard.
